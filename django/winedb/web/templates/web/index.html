Sobre este proyecto


Everyone at some point has needed to buy a wine for an occasion and nowadays is really easy to feel overwhelmed by the insane number of choices and the lack of information when entering a cellar or walking through supermarket aisles. Choosing the right one without any recommendation from cellar owner often becomes a decision based only in the wine price and the wine label.

Making a good recommendation is a hot topic in Data Science as it continues to present research challenges as well as strong practical benefits for many different areas (movies,music,restaurants,etc...). Furthermore, improving the recommendation presentation, helping users to understand, explore, and even control recommender systems has recently risen a lot attention.
